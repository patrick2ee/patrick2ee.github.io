Bio

I’ve spent the last several years building lifecycle programs that treat the marketing touchpoints like product surfaces. With deep expertise in the nuances of email code, as well as a breadth of experience in push, SMS, and in-app messages, I specialize in modular lifecycle marketing. My background spans strategy, MarTech operations, and direct collaboration with engineering and machine-learning teams.

Most recently I’ve focused on the seam between marketing and AI: rebuilding my custom library of email code as abstracted snippets to make template builds more intuitive for marketers without sacrificing their technical nuances (and making them AI-friendly). I then integrated Claude skills for automating template builds completely, making it possible to translate a batch of PDFs into production-ready code in minutes.
